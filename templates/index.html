How Calorie Targets Are Calculated

The calorie requirements are calculated using the following formulas:

  • RER (Resting Energy Requirement) = 70 √ó Body Weight(kg)0.75
  • DER (Daily Energy Requirement) = RER √ó Life Stage Factor
Life Stage Factors
  • Kittens 0‚Äì4 months: 2.5 √ó RER (rapid growth phase)
  • Kittens 4‚Äì12 months: 2.0 √ó RER (moderate growth)
  • Adult neutered: 1.2 √ó RER (maintenance)
  • Adult intact: 1.4 √ó RER (higher metabolism)
  • Adult obese-prone: 1.0 √ó RER (weight management)
Note: These are starting points. Adjust by 10‚Äì20% based on your cat's body condition and your veterinarian's advice.
